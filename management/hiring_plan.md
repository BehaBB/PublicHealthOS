## Job Descriptions & Qualifications
### Senior AI Engineer
**Requirements:**
- MS/PhD in Computer Science or related field
- 5+ years healthcare AI experience
- Expertise in Python, TensorFlow, scikit-learn
- Experience with HIPAA-compliant systems

**Key Deliverables:**
- AI risk prediction engine (Month 3)
- Model validation framework (Month 4)
- Performance monitoring system (Month 5)

### Regulatory Affairs Specialist
**Requirements:**
- Bachelor's in life sciences + regulatory certification
- 3+ years FDA 510(k) experience
- Knowledge of SaMD regulations
- Experience with eSTAR submissions

**Key Deliverables:**
- FDA Q-Sub package (Month 3)
- eSTAR documentation (Month 6)
- Quality management system (Month 8)

## Compensation Strategy
**Salary Ranges (California Market):**
- AI Engineers: $140,000 - $180,000
- Regulatory Specialists: $110,000 - $150,000
- Clinical Staff: $80,000 - $120,000
- Operations: $90,000 - $130,000

**Equity Structure:**
- Early employees: 0.5% - 2% equity
- Performance-based vesting (4 years)
- Refresh grants based on milestones

## Recruitment Channels
- **Technical Roles:** LinkedIn, GitHub, AI conferences
- **Clinical Roles:** Healthcare networks, professional associations
- **Regulatory Roles:** FDA alumni networks, compliance conferences

## Onboarding & Training
**Week 1-2:** Company orientation, system access
**Week 3-4:** Domain training (diabetes care, regulatory requirements)
**Month 2:** Full productivity expected with mentorship support
